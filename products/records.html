From the team that developed STiBaRC

Founded by Joshua Herron, Ben Border and Joseph Madril, STiBaRC Records is an independent record label primarily focused on the alternative/indie genre. The STiBaRC Records discography also has releases in the soundtrack genre, pop and country.
