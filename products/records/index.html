STiBaRC Records

A division of STiBaRC, LLC, the company behind the social media service of the same name, STiBaRC Records is an online independent record label. Blending alternative and electronic genres, STiBaRC Records is home to artists Alluthus, Bunnbuns, herronjo, and hothamms. With endless creative freedom, STiBaRC Records is dedicated to supporting artist-driven releases, catalog preservation and multimedia creativity.
