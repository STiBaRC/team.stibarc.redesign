STiBaRC

Bored of the usual social media services? Look no further, and take a gander at STiBaRC! Launching in 2017 by Joshua Herron, STiBaRC is a simplified social media service where users can create and share—from simple text posts to short-form videos and more!

Share to the Community

Create and share posts with the STiBaRC community. From short and sweet to lengthy texts, users can optionally attach images, audio files or videos for all to view on the global feed. With the 'followed' feed, you can view posts from users you exclusively follow. Made a mistake or forgot to add an attachment to your post? With the edit feature, users can edit their posts, add or delete attatchments or delete their posts.

Take a Clip

With STiBaRC Clips, share a short-form video for all to view-seperate from the main STiBaRC feed.

Engage with Others

Interact with posts and clips by leaving comments or using the up-vote, down-vote feature. Votes are private, not public, allowing users to vote on posts that best suit to their opinion.

Tons of Customization

Create a profile to your taste with a profile photo and a banner of your choosing. Additional options including a bio, pronouns, email, name and birthday are also there, all optional!

Security and Privacy

Don't want to publicly share a post or have your profile public? Users can change their account visibility, allowing only followers to view their posts. Users can make all existing posts private or keep their existing post visibility. Public profiles can optionally mark their posts as private, allowing only their followers to see the post on their feed.

Further secure your profile with two-factor authentication, adding an additional layer of security to keep your STiBaRC account safe.
