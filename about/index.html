About STiBaRC Team

The STiBaRC Team is a small group of ambitious developers focused on creating various things on the web; just for the thrill and excitement of impacting the web with our bizzare creations. Launching social media service STiBaRC in 2017, we have since ventured into other projects—from blogging service ThinkBlog and short-form video platform STiBaRC Clips to our own in-house record label STiBaRC Records. Led by founder and CEO Joshua Herron, the STiBaRC Team strives to create and develop products in our own unique, wacky way.

Timeline of STiBaRC Team

2017

STiBaRC

Joshua Herron launches STiBaRC, an easy-to-use social media site allowing users to post simple texts.

2018

STiBaRC Mobile App

STiBaRC expands to mobile devices with the launch of the STiBaRC app for mobile devices.

STiBaRC Messenger

STiBaRC Messenger launches, providing simple instant messaging to users.

First Overhaul

STiBaRC undergoes its first major overhaul for it's first anniversary, including a brand new layout and the ability to have profile photos and singular attatchments on posts.

STiBaRC|Columns

Sister site STiBaRC|Columns launches with 10 columns.

Third-party Clients

Various third party clients from members of STiBaRC Team launch.

2019

Followers and Ad System

STiBaRC adds a new follower and following feature alongside the launch of the STiBaRC Ad Portal.

STiBaBS

STiBaRC's first blog service launches.

Roomies

STiBaRC's unreleased predecessor Roomies is released.

STiBaRC Team Website Launch

An official website for STiBaRC Team launches.

2020

STiBaRC Records

STiBaRC Records launches with its first release of two EPs by Alluthus and Bunnbuns respectfully.

STiBaRC Framework

STiBaRC Team creates STiBaRC Framework, planned to be used for an overhaul of STiBaRC. This framework was later used for the STiBaRC Team website and was originally planned to be used for STiBaRC, however was later retired.

STiBaRC, LLC

STiBaRC, LLC is founded, placing products by STiBaRC Team under the company.

2021

STiBaRC Native

STiBaRC Native, a beta version of the brand new STiBaRC mobile app is released.

2022

Thinkblog

Thinkblog launches as the successor to STiBaBS.

STiBaRC Records Releases First CD

Alluthus' second album is released as STiBaRC Records' first physical product.

2023

Development of STiBaRC's New Frontend Begins

A visual overhaul of STiBaRC begins to replace the aging design from 2018.

2024

STiBaRC Beta

A new beta website showcasing STiBaRC's brand-new design and launches.

2025

Third Major STiBaRC Redesign Released

STiBaRC Team releases the third major overhaul of STiBaRC. Alongside a brand new interface, new features from multiple attatchments and private posts to additional user cutomizations with banners and 2-factor authentication are added.

STiBaRC Developer Portal

STiBaRC Team launches the STiBaRC Developer Portal and STiBaRC OAuth, allowing developers to register their applications.
